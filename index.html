Wedding Itinerary

Mehndi

An intimate afternoon of henna, music, and joy.

👗 Ladies: Green or brown matching attire.
👔 Gents: Mint colors or simple, casual pant/shirt.

Haldi

A vibrant morning of traditional rituals and blessings.

👗 Ladies: Mixed bright colors (pink, blue, orange, etc.).
👔 Gents: Traditional Kurtas.

Engagement

The official exchange of rings and promises.

👔 Attire: South Indian Veshti/Mundu and Shirt.

Sangeet & Dandiya Night

Get ready to dance the night away to joyful beats!

👗 Ladies: Chaniya Choli, Lehenga, or Western Gowns.
👔 Gents: Traditional, Indo-Western, or Kurta/Nehru Jacket.

The Wedding

The auspicious moment we tie the knot.

✨ Attire: South Indian Traditional or standard Traditional Kurta/Nehru Jacket.

Miscellaneous Events

Various rituals and relaxing gatherings.
